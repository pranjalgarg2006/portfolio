About Me

Crafting Experiences,
Not Just Interfaces

Hey! I'm Pranjal Garg, a B.Tech 2nd year student at SRM Ghaziabad with a deep passion for design that started way back in class 10 — armed with nothing but Canva and a whole lot of curiosity.

What began as casual creativity gradually became a full-blown obsession. During my second year, I dove deep into UI/UX design, discovering how design isn't just about looking good — it's about feeling right. I'm currently levelling up with a UX course on Coursera because the learning never stops.

I love both UI/UX and graphic design equally — one lets me solve problems beautifully, the other lets me tell stories visually. As an ambivert, I thrive in both collaborative sprints and deep solo design sessions.

My goal? Build a career where every pixel has purpose and every experience leaves a mark.
